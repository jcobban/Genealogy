Cobban Family History - 1999

From: Donald A. Cobban,964 Jalna Blvd,London,On,Canada N6E 2R3, Feb 4 1999

Over the past two decades, several of the family members have been involved in developing data on the family from the Caradoc Canada group and expanding backward to Scotland, to Australia, to the USA and to other areas of Canada.

Early issues of data were basically graphs and required further data to support them. Other issues made many corrections, especially in the pattern and code structure. As data was obtained from other sources such as the Mormon library, major shifts in the order of listing had to be made. Most of these corrections have been made and newer members added in the attached. (I hope)

It now is a matter of adding even newer members, rechecking the data for those already there and where possible adding some touches of history. For this purpose, the data has been assembled in family units and the history would relate to that family unit.

I would like to receive:

  • As much data as you can develop on added members
  • Check and correct or complete data on current members listed
  • Any information you may have on other groups not included with suggestions, names, addresses of possible correspondents
  • Any stories, history of family units which could be of future interest.
  • Any comments on the current method of representation and the assembly of history by individual family units

The information I receive will be added and used in a further issue of this book. hopefully in early 2000. If you can, present it in the same format as I am using or one of your own, ready for printing. After this issue, future ones will be a group at a time, prepared by the group with a copy to a central group to issue to the other groups.
