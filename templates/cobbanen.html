Cobban Family Tree

This web page documents all known descendants of John (Earnest) Cobban (1758-1813). There is also some information about his father William Couban (1724-?) and his siblings.

This web page also documents all known descendants, and ancestors, of John Wilton Sr. (1790-1861), who include my paternal grandmother Ethel Clara Wilton.

There is some information on the descendants of John Brodie (1816-1883) and Stephen Bond (1809-1886), who include my paternal great-grandmothers Margaret Brodie and Elizabeth Bond.

On my mother's side there is some information on the descendants of John Gale (about 1730-?) and William Whittaker (about 1805-?), who include my mother.

You can search for an individual in this tree by name using the Index of Names.
