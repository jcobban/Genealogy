This button is used to unlink this birth record from its current association with a record in the family tree database. Clicking on the button does not update the database, it merely changes the appearance and state of the displayed birth registration so that when you click on the Update button the database record is appropriately changed.

Note that an association between a birth registration record and an individual in the family tree may be established by adding a citation from the birth event of the individual to the birth registration record. This appears in this dialog as if the link were already recorded in the birth registration record but this sort of link cannot be cleared by clicking on this button because the link only exists in the citation record in the family tree. In that case clicking on this button will appear to make the link go away, but as soon as you redisplay this birth registration the logical link is visually reestablished. You must delete or correct the citation.
