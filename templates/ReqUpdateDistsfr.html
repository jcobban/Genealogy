The Census selection list is used to specify the year of the census enumeration for which you wish to update a District description.
The Province selection list is an alphabetical list of the provinces in the specified census. It is used if you wish to limit the displayed list of districts to a specific province. The list of districts in the province is displayed as soon as you select an entry from this list.
Click on this button to request the display of the list of districts.
