Blog Post: Help

This dialog provides the ability for anyone to create a web-log (Blog) on any subject, or to follow up a post Blog.`

You get to this dialog by selecting the hyperlink associated with a Blog on the Blogs Management Page.

If you are logged on as a registered contributor, you can edit or delete your Blogs after they are posted. The Edit button opens an instance of this page to edit the existing Blog.

Although anyone can post a Blog if you are not logged on as a registered contributor to the site you will be required to enter your e-mail address, and that e-mail address will be exposed to anyone who views your Blogs. Also if you are logged on as a registered contributor, you can edit your Blogs after they are posted.

To post a new Blog supply a subject and then use the provided rich-text editor to compose your blog just as if you were using a word processor. When you are finished just click on the Post Blog button or use the keyboard short-cut Ctrl-B.

As usual on all pages on this site popup help is provided if you position the mouse over any button or input field.

For any other issue click on the "Contact Author" hyperlink in the page footer.
