Contact Author: Help

This dialog provides the ability for a user to send a message to the owners of a particular page in the web site.

You get to this dialog by clicking on the button on the "Contact Author" link in the bottom portion of any page. Initially the dialog looks like:

It displays descriptive information about the page that referred you. This is most commonly the name of the referring script or web page, but in some cases it is a phrase with human meaning. For example if you clicked on the "Contact Author" link at the bottom of a page describing an individual in the family tree, then this descriptive phrase includes the name and dates of the individual, as well as the internal unique key.

  • If you are a casual visitor to the site, that is you are not a registered contributor, then you are asked for your e-mail address so that the author can contact you.
  • An area for entering your text message. The first part of this is pre-initialized to the descriptive text for the referencing page.

Then click on the Blog button at the bottom of the page body to send an internal message to the author or authors of the referencing page.

For any other issue click on the "Contact Author" hyperlink in the page footer.
