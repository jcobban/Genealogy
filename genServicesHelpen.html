Research Assistance: Help

This dialog provides the ability for a client to submit a basic genealogical research request. The client supplies some minimal information about the client's family, including the client's e-mail address so a response can be sent.

The page appears as a fill-in-the-blanks form covering four generations of the client's family, from the client back to the client's great-grandparents. The more information the client provides, the more research can be profitably performed.
