Ontario: Help

This page provides a list of services relating to the genealogy research in Ontario. Some of these services are provided by this web site and others by other web sites.

This page contains a link to a selection of useful web-sites providing access to genealogy information focussing on Ontario.

There is a convenience link to the extensive family tree of families of south-western Ontario.

There are links to web pages focussing on resources for the individual counties of Elgin, Lambton, and Middlesex.

This site implements tools to search and maintain free transcriptions of birth, marriage and death registrations in Ontario. While free search of these transcriptions is available to all visitors, if you register as a member you may also contribute to the expansion of the database of these "BMD" records.
