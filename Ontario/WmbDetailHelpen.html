Wesleyan Methodist Baptism Detail: Help

This page displays the complete contents of an individual baptism record your request. If the current user is authorized to update this database then the fields can be modified.

Note that one of the links in the header and footer of the page provides ability to view all of the baptisms recorded on the same page of the original record books.

Just after the page title there are three buttons:

  • A button to go to the preceding record in alphabetical order within the database.
  • A button to go to the following record in alphabetical order within the database.
  • A button to return to the original query page. There is also a hyper-link to the query page in the page header and footer.

The page displays:

Volume: The volume of original reports from which the records were transcribed.

Page: The sequential page number within a volume

District: The district name. The arrangement of districts changed from time to time since after all the political administration of the province or UpperCanada / Canada West / Ontario also changed over the years covered by the database.

Area: The lower level geographical area, typically a township for which the Minister was responsible.

Given Names The given names of the baptized person.

Family Name The surname of the baptized person.

Birth Date The date of birth, usually expressed as day, month, year.

Birth Place The place, generally the township, where the person being baptized was born.

Link This row either displays a hyperlink to the associated individual in the family tree, or else a selection list of possible matches, identified by their birth and death dates and the names of their parents.

Baptism Date The baptism date is usually expressed in the form dd month year

Baptism Place The place, generally the township, where the baptism took place

Father's Name The name, generally just the given name, of the father of the baptized person.

Mother's Name The name, generally just the given name, of the mother of the baptized person.

Parent's Residence The location, generally a township name, where the parents of the baptized person resided at the time of the baptism.

Minister The name of the presiding minister.

If you are authorized to update the table, at the bottom of the page is:

  • A button to apply any changes you have made to the record.
  • A button to clear the form.
