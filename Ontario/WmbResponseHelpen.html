Wesleyan Methodist Baptisms Query Response: Help

This page displays a summary report of records that match your request. The columns display:

Volume: The number of the microfilm volume from which the records were transcribed.

Page: The sequential page number within a volume

Surname The surname of the baptized person.

Given Names The given names of the baptized person.

Birth Place The place, generally the township, where the person being baptized was born.

Birth Date The date is expressed in the form dd month yyyy.

Details A button to display the full contents of the record.
