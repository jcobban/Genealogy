Grant Report: Help

The response displayed to you when you click on the "Grant Access" button on the grant access to Individuals page is a report listing all of the individuals for whom the access grant applies. The listing identifies the relationship between each person and the base individual, the internal unique numeric key of the person in the database, the name of the person in the tree, and the userid of the user that you granted access to. For reference purposes you might want to print a copy of this report or save a copy as a file, so you can keep track of to whom you have granted access to members of your family tree.
