This page tracks additional functionality that is being considered for inclusion in the web site. The presence of an item on this page does not imply a commitment to delivery of that functionality.

In addition to comments from the product developer this page also includes comments about the features made by users of the web site. If you wish to contribute comments you need to sign on as a registered user. If you do then a text input field and a "Post Blog" button appear after the existing comments. After typing your comments press the tab forward key to move the focus to the "Post Blog" button and then press Enter, or else use the mouse to click on the "Post Blog" button.
