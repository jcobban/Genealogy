1I am unable to identify either her husband or her son.  There is only one Sylvester Campbell in the 1852 census of Middlesex, but he has a son John who was born about 1849, too late to be the son of a wife who died in 1839.  There are two John Campbells in the 1851 census of Lobo who were born in the 1830s, but they are both living with their parents. To have erected a monument to his mother this John Campbell must have lived into adulthood and presumably, since the next line on the monument is the  recording of the death of John's grandmother, it is likely that it was the occasion of his grandmother's death that prompted commemoration of the deaths of his mother and his grandfather, so this John Campbell was presumably still alive in 1854.
