Delete Individual: Help

This page is used to report the results of a request to delete an individual from the database.

You request the deletion of an individual from the database from the Edit Individual Page where you click on the "Delete Individual" button. Note that this button is only displayed to you when the individual in question has no family connections. That is the individual is entirely unconnected to the family tree. This means that once you identify an individual as a candidate for deletion, you must first detach the individual from any spousal relationship, or relationship as a child of a family.

If you cheat and try invoking the web page explicitly, by modifying the URL in your browser to identify an individual by IDIR, then error messages are displayed. In particular an error message is displayed if the individual is not a valid candidate for deletion because of existing family relationships.

The completion of the request results in a report of how many citation records, individual event records, and individual records (1) were deleted as a result of your request.

To proceed out of this page select one of the items in the header section of the page. The most likely destination is the nominal index.
