Services de l'arbre familiale: Aide

Traduction en progres.

This page lists the functions that apply to the family tree as a whole.

  • Trouver an individual in the family tree by name. Most casual users of the family tree will go directly to this web page.
  • Ajouter un nouveau individu who is not related to any existing individual in the family tree. If the new individual is related to an existing individual, then the individual should be added as a child of an existing family, as a spouse of an existing individual, or as a parent of an existing individual. If there is no likelihood of the new individual being connected to anyone in the existing tree, you should consider creating a brand new tree.
  • Montre une liste des individuals recently added to the family tree.
  • Montre high level statistics of the database.
  • Demander un rapport of individuals in the family tree based upon many characteristics.
  • Montre source citations matching a pattern.
  • Calculate an estimated birth date for an individual based upon age at the time of an event.
  • Montre une liste des countries that are recognized by the site.
  • Montre une liste des languages that are recognized by the site.
  • Trouver et mise a jour locations that are used in the family tree. This table contains every location that is referenced by any event in the family tree.
  • Trouver et mise a jour temples of the Church of Latter Day Saints that are referenced by events in the family tree.
  • Trouver et mise a jour master source records that are referenced by citations in the family tree. A master source is a resource, whether conventional or electronic, that contains documentary evidence supporting the facts recorded in the tree.
  • Trouver et mise a jour address records that contain detailed information about how to communicate with individuals or institutions connected to the family tree, or as a more detailed substitute for a location record.
