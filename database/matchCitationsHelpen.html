Census: Match Citations Help

This page reports the results of attempting to match citations from the family tree to the current page of the census.

The script searches for all citations from individuals to the page identified by year of census, province for pre-confederation censuses, district number, sub-district identifier, division if applicable, and page number. Each individual with such a citation should correspond to an individual on this page. An attempt is made to match the individual by name, including married name if applicable, gender, and approximate age. If a match is found it is reported on this page, and the database record for the associated line is updated to contain a linkage to the family tree record.

You can close this page at any time after it is displayed, as there are no further actions to perform.

When you look back at the census form for the associated page you will observe that the Find button for each identified match has been changed to a Show button, and if you click on it the associated family tree individual is displayed in a new tab or window.
