British Columbia: Help

This page provides a list of services relating to the genealogy research in British Columbia. Some of these services are provided by this web site and others by other web sites.

This page contains a link to a selection of useful web-sites providing access to genealogy information focussing on British Columbia.

This site implements tools to search and maintain free transcriptions of birth, marriage and death registrations in British Columbia. While free search of these transcriptions is available to all visitors, if you register as a member you may also contribute to the expansion of the database of these "BMD" records.
