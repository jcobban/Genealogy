Birth Registration Status: Help

The associated panel displays a high-level overview of the progress of the transcription effort for birth registrations.

For each registration year this page displays the number of birth registrations that have been transcribed. To be considered as transcribed at least the name of the child has been transcribed. Clicking on the View button associated with the years shows a more detailed breakdown of the transcriptions for that particular year.
